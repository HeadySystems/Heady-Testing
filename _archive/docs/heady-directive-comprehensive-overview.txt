PHI-BASE OPTIMIZATION (GOLDEN RATIO)
----------------------------------------

Replace all base-2 math in system with phi-base (1.618...):

  Attempt | 2^(n-1) | phi^(n-1)
  --------+---------+----------
  1       | 1.0     | 1.0
  2       | 2.0     | 1.618
  3       | 4.0     | 2.618
  4       | 8.0     | 4.236
  5       | 16.0    | 6.854
  6       | 32.0    | 11.090

  * Phi grows slower than 2 = more aggressive early retries
  * Better recovery for transient failures
  * Keep max, stop conditions, and jitter in place

Applications: Backoff delays, scaling decisions, retry logic, resource
allocation, decay functions, growth parameters.
