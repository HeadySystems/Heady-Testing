HeadyCoin

Community Token Economy

HeadyCoin powers governance, compute credits, and marketplace transactions within the HeadyOS ecosystem.

Token Utility

HeadyCoin (HCN) is the native utility token of the HeadyOS ecosystem, enabling decentralized governance, compute credit allocation, and frictionless marketplace transactions.

  • Governance voting on protocol upgrades and parameter changes
  • Compute credits for running HeadyOS agent workloads
  • Marketplace transactions for nodes, templates, and integrations
  • Staking rewards for long-term community participants
  • Priority access to new features and beta programs

Community Guidelines

Our Community Standards

Building a welcoming, productive community for everyone exploring autonomous AI.

🤝

Respect & Inclusion

Treat all members with respect. We welcome builders of all backgrounds and experience levels. No harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary behavior.

🔬

Technical Excellence

Share knowledge generously. Provide constructive feedback. Credit others' work. Cite sources when discussing research or benchmarks.

🔒

Privacy & Security

Protect confidential information. Do not share API keys, credentials, or private data. Report security vulnerabilities responsibly through proper channels.
