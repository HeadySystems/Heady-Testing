Our Journey

Milestones in the evolution of HeadyOS and Sacred Geometry v4.0

Q1 2024
HeadySystems Founded
Eric Haywood establishes HeadySystems Inc. with a vision to bring sacred geometry principles to computational orchestration. The first architectural sketches incorporate PHI ratios and Fibonacci sequences into system design.
Q2 2024
Sacred Geometry v1.0
Initial release of sacred geometry design system. Implementation of golden ratio proportions, Fibonacci spacing, and harmonic color palettes across desktop interface.
Q3 2024
HCFullPipeline Launch
Deployment of intelligent pipeline orchestration engine. First implementation of adaptive parallelization and sacred geometry-informed optimization algorithms in workflow execution.
Q4 2024
HeadyBee Swarm Beta
Launch of distributed worker coordination system. Swarm intelligence protocols enable massively parallel task execution with organic load balancing inspired by natural systems.
Q1 2025
MCP Integration & 20 Patents
Full Model Context Protocol integration. Integration of Anthropic's MCP standard for seamless AI interoperability. 20 provisional patents filed covering core innovations.
Q2 2025
Liquid Nodes Framework
Release of universal protocol translation framework enabling flexible compute and integration nodes. HeadyTranslator enables seamless communication across MCP, HTTP, WebSocket, UDP, MIDI, and TCP.
Q3 2025
Sacred Geometry v4.0 & 51 Patents
Latest iteration of sacred geometry design system with enhanced PHI-ratio optimization and Fibonacci-based adaptive spacing. 51 provisional patents now filed across all major system components.
Q4 2025
Platform Expansion
Mobile application launch, enterprise feature suite expansion, and integration with Google Colab Pro+ for extended computational capabilities. System enters production at scale.
