Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about HeadyOS plans, billing, and features. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime? +
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading mid-cycle, you'll pay a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing period. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers for annual plans, and custom payment arrangements for Enterprise customers. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
Is there a free trial for Professional? +
Absolutely! Start with our Starter plan forever free to explore all HeadyOS capabilities. When ready, upgrade to Professional to unlock unlimited pipeline runs, additional Liquid Nodes, and priority support. You'll only pay when you're ready.
What happens if I exceed my plan limits? +
For Starter plans, pipeline runs pause after hitting the daily limit. You'll be notified and can upgrade anytime to continue. Professional and Enterprise customers have flexible capacity that scales automatically. We monitor usage and alert you if approaching vector operation limits.
Do you offer team or organization discounts? +
Yes! Teams of 5+ users can earn volume discounts on Professional plans. Enterprise customers receive custom pricing based on deployment size and requirements. Contact our sales team for a personalized quote.
What's included in Enterprise support? +
Enterprise support includes a dedicated account manager, 24/7 phone and email support, response time SLA (1 hour for critical issues), custom feature development, and priority bug fixes. You also get on-site support and training options available.
Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied? +
Start with Starter forever free to ensure HeadyOS is right for you. If you upgrade and change your mind within 30 days, we offer a full refund. Enterprise customers work directly with our team to find the right solution.
Is billing monthly or annual? What's the difference? +
Professional plans are $49/month billed monthly, or $490/year (2 months free) when billed annually. Enterprise customers receive custom invoicing terms. Annual billing provides better value and ensures uninterrupted service.
How does vector operation usage work? +
Vector operations power latent space reasoning, semantic search, and AI-driven decision making. Each operation in your workflows (embedding calculations, similarity searches, etc.) counts toward your monthly quota. You can monitor usage in your MCP Dashboard. Unused operations don't roll over to the next month.
