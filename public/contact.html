Frequently Asked Questions

What is HeadyOS? ▼
HeadyOS is the world's first operating system built on Sacred Geometry principles. It uses mathematical constants like PHI, PSI, and Fibonacci sequences to optimize resource allocation, scaling, and system behavior — replacing arbitrary "magic numbers" with universal mathematical principles found throughout nature.
How is HeadyOS different from traditional operating systems? ▼
Traditional OSes use trial-and-error tuning and arbitrary constants. HeadyOS integrates Sacred Geometry principles at every layer — from the 14-stage HCFullPipeline to resource scheduling. This makes systems more elegant, resilient, and aligned with natural patterns.
Is HeadyOS ready for production use? ▼
HeadyOS v3.0.0 is currently available and includes Sacred Geometry v4.0, HeadySwarm, and Liquid Nodes. We recommend starting with our community edition to evaluate compatibility with your infrastructure before enterprise deployment.
What platforms does HeadyOS support? ▼
HeadyOS runs on Linux, macOS, and Windows. We also provide Kubernetes manifests and Docker containers for cloud deployments. Specialized builds are available for embedded systems and edge computing platforms.
How do I get started with HeadyOS? ▼
Visit our documentation at docs.headysystems.com to download the installer. We provide step-by-step setup guides, video tutorials, and a community Discord channel where you can ask questions and connect with other users.
Is HeadyOS open source? ▼
Core components of HeadyOS are open source under the GNU General Public License (GPL). Enterprise features are available under commercial licensing. We believe in open architecture and community contributions.
How is HeadyOS secured against quantum computing threats? ▼
HeadyOS implements post-quantum cryptography using lattice-based encryption and hash-based signatures. These algorithms are resilient against attacks from both classical and quantum computers, protecting your data for decades to come.
What is the CodeLock system? ▼
CodeLock is HeadyOS's zero-trust file integrity and approval workflow system. It monitors all code changes, maintains cryptographic integrity hashes, and requires explicit approvals before deploying code — preventing unauthorized modifications and ensuring audit compliance.
How can I report security vulnerabilities? ▼
Please email security@headysystems.com with a detailed description of the vulnerability. Do not publicly disclose the issue. Our security team will respond within 24 hours and work with you on a coordinated disclosure timeline.
What kind of support is available? ▼
We offer community support via GitHub Issues, Discord, and email. Enterprise customers receive dedicated support engineers, SLA guarantees, and priority response times. Contact business@headysystems.com for enterprise support pricing.
