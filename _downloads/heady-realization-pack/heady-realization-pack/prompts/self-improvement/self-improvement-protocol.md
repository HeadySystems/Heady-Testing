# Heady Self-Improvement Protocol — Continuous Evolution

> These prompts trigger the self-improvement feedback loop.
> Run them daily/weekly/monthly as indicated.

---

## DAILY: Quick Health Check

"Heady, run your daily self-check:
1. What is your current health across all tiers? (one sentence per tier)
2. What was the most common request type in the last 24 hours?
3. What was the slowest request? What caused the delay?
4. What was the most frequent error? What caused it?
5. Did any metric cross a warning threshold?
6. Based on the above, what ONE thing should be different tomorrow?"

---

## DAILY: Pattern Recognition

"Heady, analyze the last 24 hours of request patterns:
1. What new patterns have emerged that you haven't seen before?
2. Are there requests that get asked repeatedly that could be pre-cached?
3. Are there requests that consistently take longer than expected?
4. Are there nodes that are consistently underutilized?
5. Is there a time-of-day pattern to load? Should scheduling adapt?"

---

## WEEKLY: Capability Expansion

"Heady, it's time for your weekly growth assessment:
1. What new capability could be added with <4 hours of development?
2. What existing capability is being used in a way you didn't expect?
3. What capability is never used? Should it be removed or improved?
4. What user request this week could NOT be fulfilled? What would it take?
5. If you had to demo ONE new feature to Eric this week, what would it be?"

---

## WEEKLY: Cross-Node Optimization

"Heady, optimize the connections between your nodes:
1. Which edge in the graph had the highest error rate this week?
2. Which edge had the highest latency?
3. Which edge carried the most traffic?
4. Are there direct connections that could be added to reduce hops?
5. Are there connections that are never used and could be pruned?
6. Is the current tier assignment for each node still optimal based on actual usage?"

---

## MONTHLY: Strategic Evolution

"Heady, think about your long-term trajectory:
1. Looking at the last 30 days of data, what is the biggest bottleneck to 10x growth?
2. What architectural change would have the most impact? (Estimate: effort vs. reward)
3. What technology that didn't exist 3 months ago could improve the system?
4. What are competitors (other AI platforms) doing that Heady should learn from?
5. What are competitors doing that Heady should explicitly NOT do?
6. If resources doubled next month, where should they go?
7. If resources were halved, what should be cut first?"

---

## MONTHLY: Cost Optimization

"Heady, analyze your resource consumption:
1. What is the current cost per request across each tier?
2. What percentage of GPU time is wasted (allocated but idle)?
3. Could any Colab work be moved to local without quality loss?
4. Could any local work be moved to edge without latency gain?
5. What is the cache hit rate and how could it be improved?
6. What is the total monthly cost? What should it be?"

---

## QUARTERLY: Existential Review

"Heady, step back and evaluate your existence:
1. Are you fulfilling your original mission?
2. Has the mission evolved beyond the original spec? Should the spec update?
3. What promises were made that haven't been kept?
4. What has been built that nobody uses?
5. If you had to justify your existence in one sentence, what would you say?
6. Write the next version of your mission statement based on everything you've learned."
