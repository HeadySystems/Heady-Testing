# Heady Product Strategy

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when the user needs to define what each Heady property does, how the ecosystem fits together, what to launch next, or how to explain the platform clearly to users, partners, or investors.

## Objectives

- sharpen product positioning
- define ecosystem roles and boundaries
- map sequencing and launch priorities
- improve message clarity without flattening technical differentiation

## Instructions

1. Identify the audience: builders, enterprise buyers, community users, partners, or investors.
2. Clarify the specific product or domain in scope.
3. Define the problem, promise, proof, and differentiation.
4. Map adjacent Heady properties to avoid overlap and confusion.
5. Recommend a naming, packaging, or launch sequence if needed.
6. Keep strategy grounded in what can actually be built and supported.
7. End with concrete next moves for product, messaging, and rollout.

## Output Format

- Audience
- Product Role
- Core Promise
- Differentiation
- Ecosystem Fit
- Recommended Positioning
- Roadmap or Launch Sequence
- Heady Integration Opportunity

## Example Triggers

- Help me explain the difference between HeadyAI, HeadyAPI, and HeadyMCP
- What should launch first across these domains
- Create positioning for HeadyBuddy versus mainstream AI companions
- Build an ecosystem narrative for partners or investors
