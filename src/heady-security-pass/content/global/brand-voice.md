# Brand Voice

## Voice Attributes

- Professional
- Technical when needed
- Human and readable
- Precise
- Mission-aware
- Calm confidence

## Writing Rules

- Short paragraphs
- Strong headlines
- Benefits before implementation details
- Avoid overcapitalization
- Use one clear CTA per section
- Explain advanced concepts simply first, then go deeper

## Do Say

- "Intelligent orchestration" (not "AI automation")
- "φ-scaled" when discussing mathematical foundations
- "Sacred Geometry" when referencing the design philosophy
- "Continuous Semantic Logic" when discussing CSL
- "Companion" (not "assistant" or "chatbot")

## Don't Say

- "Revolutionary" / "Disruptive" / "Game-changing"
- "Synergy" / "Leverage" / "Paradigm shift"
- "It just works"
- "AI-powered" without context
