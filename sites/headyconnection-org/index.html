Our Mission

Bridge the AI divide and ensure that everyone—regardless of income, background, or geography—has access to transformative artificial intelligence tools, education, and opportunities.

What We Believe

  • AI is a public good, not a luxury
  • Technology must serve humanity, not divide it
  • Communities should guide AI development for themselves
  • Knowledge is power—education changes lives
  • Diversity of thought strengthens AI systems
  • Open-source tools empower independent innovation

The AI Divide

Today, AI access is concentrated in wealthy nations and corporations. Billions of people worldwide lack:

  • Basic AI literacy and understanding
  • Access to modern AI tools and services
  • Representation in AI training data
  • Voice in algorithmic decision-making
  • Economic opportunities in AI economy
  • Protection from AI-driven discrimination

Our Approach

We work directly with communities to understand their needs, provide accessible education, offer free tools and resources, fund grassroots innovation, and ensure community voices shape AI development.
