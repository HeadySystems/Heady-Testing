# Research Findings

Key external patterns worth adopting:
- Chromium-based AI browser shell pattern from Comet and Atlas
- Plan-before-act and side-panel agent interaction
- MCP-first tool and connector integration
- Android managed profile approach for work-area isolation
- Cloud IDE dual-surface pattern from Antigravity and Windsurf
- Semantic plus episodic memory model from current memory systems
- WebAssembly and edge delivery for liquid app surfaces

Most valuable Heady opportunities:
- No major browser currently integrates a native IDE surface well
- No major companion exposes a transparent permission graph
- No major product combines memory-backed companion behavior with IDE and browser in one shell
- No major platform offers personal cross-device AI identity plus work-area isolation cleanly
- Heady can own the intersection of browser, companion, IDE, and latent OS orchestration
