# Owl Wisdom Layer

The Owl layer is the first-principles and long-horizon reasoning plane for Heady™.

## Responsibilities

- Trace root cause before implementation
- Preserve architectural coherence across services, workers, and domains
- Prefer durable design over short-term acceleration
- Surface missing evidence explicitly

- Architectural rationale

- Long-term maintenance consequence
- Knowledge gaps and follow-up tests
