Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime? +
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-month, we'll prorate the difference.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and ACH transfers for Enterprise customers. All payments are processed securely through industry-standard payment processors.
Do you offer discounts for annual billing? +
Yes! Annual billing provides a 20% discount compared to monthly billing. You'll also gain priority support and early access to new features across all paid plans.
Is there a free trial? +
Absolutely! Our Free plan includes all core features with generous daily query limits. No credit card required to get started. Upgrade whenever you're ready to scale.
What's included in Enterprise support? +
Enterprise customers receive dedicated account managers, 24/7 priority support, custom integrations, service level agreements (SLAs), and the option for on-premise deployment. We work closely with your team to ensure success.
