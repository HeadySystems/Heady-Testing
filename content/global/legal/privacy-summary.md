# Privacy Summary

© 2026 HeadyConnection — All Rights Reserved

## Data Collection

Heady collects only the data necessary to deliver intelligent services. This includes:

- User-provided input (messages, preferences, settings)
- Usage analytics (anonymized, aggregated)
- Device and session information (for cross-device sync)

## Data Storage

- All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Vector memory stored in PostgreSQL with pgvector (Google Cloud SQL)
- Edge data processed on Cloudflare Workers (no persistent storage)

## Data Use

- Your data powers your personal AI companion experience
- We do not sell personal data to third parties
- We do not train external AI models on your personal data

## User Control

- Export your data at any time via the HeadyMe dashboard
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Control what HeadyBuddy remembers and forgets
