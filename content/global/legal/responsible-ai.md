# Responsible AI Commitment

## Principles

1. **Transparency** — Users understand when they interact with AI and how decisions are made
2. **Fairness** — Systems designed to avoid bias and ensure equitable outcomes
3. **Safety** — CSL confidence gates prevent low-quality outputs from reaching users
4. **Privacy** — Data minimization and user control over personal information
5. **Accountability** — Clear audit trails and human oversight

## Implementation

- All AI outputs pass through CSL confidence gates (threshold ≥ φ⁻¹ ≈ 0.618)
- Bias detection via HeadyAnalyze on training data and outputs
- Transparent model attribution — users know which AI model generated each response
- User feedback loops for continuous improvement
- Regular third-party audits of AI systems
