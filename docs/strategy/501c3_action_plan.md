## Executive Summary

HeadyConnection.org operates as the community mission arm of the HeadySystems ecosystem.
Formalizing as a 501(c)(3) public charity unlocks:

1. **Tax-exempt status** — no federal income tax on program revenue
2. **Donor deductibility** — individual and corporate donors can deduct contributions
3. **Grant eligibility** — NSF, NEA, IMLS, foundation grants require 501(c)(3) status
4. **Credibility** — institutional partners require nonprofit status for MOU/partnership
5. **Separation of mission from commercial** — clean firewall between HeadySystems Inc.
   (C-Corp, commercial) and HeadyConnection (nonprofit, mission)

## 1023-EZ Eligibility Checklist

HeadyConnection qualifies for Form 1023-EZ (streamlined application) if ALL of the
following are true:

| Criterion | Status | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Annual gross receipts ≤ $50,000 (projected 3 years) | ✅ | Pre-revenue |
| Total assets ≤ $250,000 | ✅ | No significant assets |
| Not a church, school, or hospital | ✅ |
| Not a foreign organization | ✅ | US entity |
| Not previously revoked exempt status | ✅ | New entity |
| Incorporated in a US state | ⬜ | **Required — see Step 1** |
| EIN obtained | ⬜ | **Required — see Step 2** |

**Filing fee:** $275 (reduced fee for organizations with gross receipts ≤ $10K)
**Processing time:** 2-4 weeks (1023-EZ online, irs.gov)

## Step-by-Step Filing Plan

### Step 1: State Incorporation (Week 1)

**Recommended state:** Colorado (where operations are based)
**Entity type:** Nonprofit Corporation

```
Colorado Secretary of State — Articles of Incorporation
Online filing: sos.state.co.us
Fee: $50
Processing: 1-2 business days

Required fields:
- Entity name: HeadyConnection Inc.
- Principal office: [Eric's address]
- Registered agent: [Eric Haywood or registered agent service]
- Purpose clause (CRITICAL — must satisfy IRS):
  "HeadyConnection Inc. is organized exclusively for charitable,
   educational, and scientific purposes under Section 501(c)(3)
   of the Internal Revenue Code. The corporation's specific
   purpose is to democratize access to artificial intelligence
   tools and education for underserved and under-resourced
   communities."
- Dissolution clause (CRITICAL — required for 501(c)(3)):
  "Upon dissolution, remaining assets shall be distributed to
   one or more organizations exempt under Section 501(c)(3) of
   the Internal Revenue Code."
```

**Bylaws required items for IRS approval:**
- [ ] Statement of purpose (match Articles above)
- [ ] Board composition (minimum 3 directors, majority independent)
- [ ] Conflict of interest policy (IRS Form 1023-EZ requires affirmation)
- [ ] Compensation policy (no private inurement clause)
- [ ] Meeting and quorum requirements
- [ ] Amendment procedure

### Step 2: Obtain EIN (Week 1, same day as incorporation)

```
IRS EIN Application — Online (immediate)
irs.gov → Employer Identification Number → Apply Online
Entity type: Corporation
Reason: Started a new business
State: Colorado

Takes 5 minutes. EIN issued immediately online.
```

### Step 3: Open Nonprofit Bank Account (Week 1-2)

Recommended: **Relay Financial** or **Mercury** (nonprofit-friendly, no fees)
Requires: Articles of Incorporation + EIN + Board resolution

### Step 4: Board Composition (Week 1-2)

Minimum 3 directors. To qualify as public charity (not private foundation), majority
must be independent (no family relationships, no financial interest in HeadySystems Inc.).

**Recommended initial board:**
- Eric Haywood (Founder, Director) — non-independent
- 2× community members (independent directors)
  - Suggested: AI ethics researcher, community organizer, or educator

### Step 5: File Form 1023-EZ (Week 2-3)

```
IRS.gov → Tax Exempt Organizations → Form 1023-EZ
File online via Pay.gov
Fee: $275

Key attestations required:
✅ Organized and operate exclusively for exempt purposes
✅ Not a private foundation (public charity)
✅ Assets will not benefit private shareholders or individuals
✅ Will not violate political campaign prohibition (Section 501(c)(3))
✅ Will not substantially engage in lobbying
✅ Compliant with public disclosure requirements
✅ Conflict of interest policy adopted
```

### Step 6: State Tax Exemption (Week 4)

After IRS determination letter received:
```
Colorado Department of Revenue
Form DR 0715 — Application for Sales Tax Exemption
Fee: $0
Processing: 2-3 weeks
```

## Revenue Covenant Language

The following covenant must appear in:
1. HeadyConnection bylaws
2. Any partnership agreements with HeadySystems Inc.
3. Annual board resolutions

```
HEADY REVENUE COVENANT

HeadySystems Inc. ("Commercial Entity") and HeadyConnection Inc.
("Mission Entity") operate under the following covenant:

1. PLATFORM ACCESS: HeadySystems shall provide HeadyConnection with
   access to HeadyMCP platform tools at no cost for mission-aligned
   programs serving underserved communities.

2. REVENUE ALLOCATION: HeadySystems shall direct no less than 5%
   of annual net revenue to HeadyConnection's grant fund, once
   HeadySystems achieves $500,000 annual recurring revenue.

3. INDEPENDENCE: HeadyConnection's board maintains full independence
   in grant-making decisions. HeadySystems may not direct
   HeadyConnection's charitable expenditures.

4. TRANSPARENCY: HeadySystems shall publish an annual impact report
   documenting communities served, AI access provided, and
   educational programs funded through HeadyConnection.

5. NON-INUREMENT: No board member, officer, or founder of either
   entity shall receive private benefit from HeadyConnection's
   assets beyond reasonable compensation for services rendered.

This covenant is binding upon both entities and their successors.
```

## Post-Filing Obligations

Once 501(c)(3) status granted:

- **Annual Form 990-N** (e-Postcard) — required for organizations with <$50K gross receipts
- **Public disclosure** — Form 990 and determination letter must be publicly available
- **Board meetings** — minimum 1/year, minutes required
- **Conflict of interest policy** — annual signed disclosure from each director
- **No private inurement** — any services from HeadySystems to HeadyConnection must be
  documented at fair market value
