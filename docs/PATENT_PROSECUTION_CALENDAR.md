## Overview

Provisional patent applications expire **12 months from the filing date**. Failure to file a non-provisional application (or PCT) before the deadline results in permanent loss of the priority date. This calendar tracks the 12 patent lock zones (HS-2026-051 through HS-2026-062) and their prosecution timelines.

Per the Innovation Timeline in PATENT_MAP.md, early patent filings (Patents 1-51) began in **2023 Q1-Q3**. The HS-051 through HS-062 series (Edge & Security family) includes the latest batch filings. Several of these patents have USPTO serial numbers in the 63/998,7xx range, confirming provisional filing status.
