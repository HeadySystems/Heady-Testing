# Incident Notification Templates

## Internal incident declaration
Subject: Incident declared - [SEVERITY] - [SYSTEM]

Summary:
- Time detected:
- Systems affected:
- Suspected data involved:
- Current containment status:
- Incident lead:
- Next update time:

## Regulator assessment template
- Incident description
- Categories and approximate number of affected individuals
- Categories and approximate number of affected records
- Likely consequences
- Measures taken or proposed
- Contact point for follow-up
- Decision on whether notice is required and why

## Customer or user notice template
Subject: Notice of a security incident affecting your information

Hello,

We are writing to inform you of a security incident that affected [SYSTEM / SERVICE]. We identified the issue on [DATE] and took steps to contain and investigate it.

Information involved: [CATEGORIES]
What we did: [CONTAINMENT / REMEDIATION]
What you can do: [USER GUIDANCE]
How to contact us: [CONTACT]

## Vendor follow-up template
Subject: Urgent security incident information request

Please provide the following immediately:
- Timeline of discovery and containment
- Systems and data categories affected
- Number of impacted records or users
- Root cause if known
- Remediation actions taken
- Whether any subprocessors were involved
- Point of contact for ongoing coordination

## Framework basis
- NIST incident response lifecycle: https://nvlpubs.nist.gov/nistpubs/SpecialPublications/NIST.SP.800-61r2.pdf
- GDPR breach notification obligations: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2016/679/oj/eng
