# Notice at Collection Draft

Use this notice at or before the point where personal information is collected.

## What we collect
We may collect identifiers and contact details, account credentials or authentication signals, billing information, support communications, device and usage information, diagnostics, and content submitted through service features.

## Why we collect it
We collect this information to provide and secure the services, manage accounts, process payments, respond to support requests, prevent abuse, improve performance, and meet legal obligations.

## Retention
We retain each category only for as long as reasonably necessary for the disclosed purposes, including security, accounting, dispute-resolution, and legal-hold needs.

## Rights
Depending on location, you may have rights to know, access, delete, correct, or opt out of certain processing. Provide a URL or email for requests here.

## Drafting notes
- If sensitive personal information is collected, add the required limitation disclosure and explain operational use.
- If data is used for targeted advertising, sharing, or sale as defined by applicable law, add the required opt-out language and links.
- Keep this notice aligned with the final Privacy Policy and actual product flows.
