# DSAR Intake and Response Templates

## Intake fields
- Request date
- Requester name
- Email and account identifier
- Jurisdiction or residence
- Request type: access / delete / correct / portability / opt-out / restrict / object
- Product or service involved
- Verification method used
- Systems searched
- Deadline and internal owner

## Acknowledgment email template
Subject: We received your privacy request

Hello,

We received your privacy request on [DATE]. We are reviewing your request and, where necessary, will verify your identity before taking action. We will respond within the time period required by applicable law.

If we need additional information to verify or clarify your request, we will let you know.

Regards,
[TEAM]

## Extension notice template
Subject: Update on your privacy request

Hello,

We are still processing your request and need additional time due to [REASON]. We expect to complete our response by [DATE].

Regards,
[TEAM]

## Fulfillment template
Subject: Response to your privacy request

Hello,

We completed our review of your request. The action taken is summarized below:
- Request type: [TYPE]
- Action taken: [ACTION]
- Date completed: [DATE]
- Additional notes: [NOTES]

Regards,
[TEAM]

## Partial denial template
Subject: Response to your privacy request

Hello,

We reviewed your request. We were able to complete part of the request, but certain information or actions could not be provided because [LEGAL / SECURITY / RIGHTS-OF-OTHERS REASON].

If applicable, you may have the right to appeal or complain to the relevant authority.

Regards,
[TEAM]
