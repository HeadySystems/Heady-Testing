FAQ

Common questions

What is the 13-day free trial?
All paid plans include a 13-day free trial (fib(7)=13) with full feature access. No credit card required to start. You can cancel at any time during the trial period and will not be charged. If you don't cancel, billing begins automatically after the trial ends.
What does "Bring Your Own Model" (BYOM) mean?
BYOM allows you to connect any compatible LLM endpoint (Ollama, vLLM, OpenAI-compatible API, Anthropic, or custom models) to the Heady™ Latent OS runtime. When using BYOM, token consumption is not metered by Heady™ — you pay your LLM provider directly and Heady charges nothing for inference. Enterprise plans with BYOM have completely unlimited usage with no token caps or overage charges. This is particularly valuable for regulated industries where data must never leave your infrastructure.
How does the annual discount work?
Annual billing provides a 21% discount (fib(8)=21) on all base plan prices. Developer drops from $29/mo to $23/mo ($276/year, saving $72). Team drops from $99/seat/mo to $78/seat/mo ($936/seat/year, saving $252/seat). Usage-based overages are still billed monthly regardless of billing cycle. You can switch from monthly to annual at any time; the difference will be prorated on your next invoice.
What's the difference between HeadyConnection and HeadySystems?
HeadyConnection is our nonprofit arm that maintains the open-source Community edition under the MIT license. It runs community programs, developer education, and open governance. HeadySystems is the commercial entity that operates the hosted platform, sells enterprise licenses, and funds ongoing R&D. Revenue from Heady™Systems supports HeadyConnection's open-source mission.
Do usage overages cause service interruption?
No. By default, overages are billed at the rates shown in the add-ons section. You can set hard limits per user or organization from the admin dashboard to prevent unexpected charges. When a hard limit is reached, affected operations return a 429 rate-limit error rather than incurring overage. Enterprise plans can configure custom overage behavior including alerts, throttling, or graceful degradation.
Is there a nonprofit or open-source discount?
Yes. Verified 501(c)(3) nonprofits, educational institutions, and open-source projects with 100+ GitHub stars receive a 30% discount on Developer and Team plans. Contact hello@headyconnection.org with your organization details to apply. Research institutions and NGOs working on public-interest AI applications may qualify for additional credits.
What compliance certifications are included with Enterprise?
Enterprise contracts include SOC 2 Type II coverage, ISO 27001, and HIPAA BAA availability. FedRAMP Moderate is on our roadmap. On-premises deployments help customers achieve their own compliance requirements since Heady's infrastructure is not in scope for their audits. We provide detailed security documentation, vendor questionnaire responses, and security review sessions for enterprise procurement teams.
Can I migrate from Community (self-hosted) to a paid cloud plan?
Yes. The Latent OS runtime uses a portable state format. You can export your vector memory, agent configurations, and MCP tool definitions from a self-hosted Community deployment and import them directly into a cloud-hosted Developer or Team account. The migration wizard in the admin panel automates this process. Enterprise customers get dedicated migration support including professional services for complex on-premises-to-cloud or cloud-to-on-premises migrations.
