## How to Use This Template

Complete one copy of this template per patent/invention before non-provisional filing. The goal is to:
1. Identify blocking prior art early (before investing in prosecution costs)
2. Document differentiation arguments that will be used in the specification's Background section
3. Identify CPC classification codes for the examiner's search
4. Build a searchable record of your prior art investigation

**Recommended timing**: Complete 60–90 days before targeted non-provisional filing date.
